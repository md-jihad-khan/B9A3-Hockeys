Ski Touring

Ski touring, also known as backcountry skiing, is a form of skiing that combines elements of downhill skiing and cross-country skiing. Unlike traditional skiing, ski touring involves traveling over varied and ungroomed terrain, often in remote and mountainous areas. It's an adventurous and physically demanding activity that attracts outdoor enthusiasts seeking a unique winter experience.
